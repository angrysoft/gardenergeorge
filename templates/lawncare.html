Lawn Care :

Mowing Services

We will cut the grass and we can collect clippings or mulch it. Clippings will be blown off hard surface areas.

Fertilization

Systematic fertilization of an established lawn is important because it keeps the lawn looking good, it will reduces weeds.

Broad Leaf Weed Control

On every visit we will do spot check for weeds. Once identified, we apply a treatment, enough fast that we can avoid spread it through your lawn.

Aeration and Detaching

Aeration and detaching important component in a healthy lawn. Aeration allows water, air and nutrients to reach the root system of you lawn. Detaching will remove thatch which acts as a barrier to water, air and nutrient getting to the roots of the grass. Those treatments should be done minimum once a year and twice when grass clippings are mulched not collected or heavy traffic on lawn is present.

Overseeding

Overseeding is necessary in order to increase the density of lawn. Most of the lawn overseeding we do, is done after Aeration and Detaching. Grass seeds can get to the holes which were done by Aerator and rows done by Scarifier. We usually offer Topdressing the lawn as well with this service. In this process we add a fine layer of : mixed quality, sand when is problem with driainage or fine compost when you have poor quality top soil.
